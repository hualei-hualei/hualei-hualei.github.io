    f′(x)=lim⁡△x→0f(x+△x)−f(x)△x=dydx\begin{equation} \begin{aligned} f'(x) &= \lim_{\triangle x \to 0} \frac{f(x + \triangle x) - f(x)} {\triangle x} \\ &= \frac{dy}{dx} \end{aligned} \end{equation}f′(x)​=△x→0lim​△xf(x+△x)−f(x)​=dxdy​​​​

  • x∈Rm\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^{m}x∈Rm为向量（标量-向量）
    记f(x)=f(x1,x2,⋯ ,xm)f(\mathbf{x}) = f(x_1, x_2, \cdots, x_m)f(x)=f(x1​,x2​,⋯,xm​)
    dfdx=[∂f∂x1∂f∂x2⋮∂f∂xm]=▽f\begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \frac{df}{d \mathbf{x}} &= \left[ \begin{array}{c} \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_1}\\ \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_2}\\ \vdots \\ \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_m} \end{array} \right] \\ &= \triangledown f \end{aligned} \end{equation}dxdf​​=⎣⎡​∂x1​∂f​∂x2​∂f​⋮∂xm​∂f​​⎦⎤​=▽f​​​
    ▽f\triangledown f▽f称为f(x)f(\mathbf{x})f(x)的梯度。

  • x∈Rp×q\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^{p \times q}x∈Rp×q为矩阵
    df(x)dx=[df(x)dx11df(x)dx12⋯df(x)dx1qdf(x)dx21df(x)dx22⋯df(x)dx2q⋮⋮⋮⋮df(x)dxp1df(x)dxp2⋯df(x)dxpq]\begin{equation} \frac{df(\mathbf{x})}{d \mathbf{x}} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} \frac{df(\mathbf{x})}{d\mathbf{x}_{11}} & \frac{df(\mathbf{x})}{d\mathbf{x}_{12}} & \cdots & \frac{df(\mathbf{x})}{d\mathbf{x}_{1q}} \\ \frac{df(\mathbf{x})}{d\mathbf{x}_{21}} & \frac{df(\mathbf{x})}{d\mathbf{x}_{22}} & \cdots & \frac{df(\mathbf{x})}{d\mathbf{x}_{2q}} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\ \frac{df(\mathbf{x})}{d\mathbf{x}_{p1}} & \frac{df(\mathbf{x})}{d\mathbf{x}_{p2}} & \cdots & \frac{df(\mathbf{x})}{d\mathbf{x}_{pq}} \end{array} \right] \end{equation}dxdf(x)​=⎣⎡​dx11​df(x)​dx21​df(x)​⋮dxp1​df(x)​​dx12​df(x)​dx22​df(x)​⋮dxp2​df(x)​​⋯⋯⋮⋯​dx1q​df(x)​dx2q​df(x)​⋮dxpq​df(x)​​⎦⎤​​​

2. f(x)∈Rnf(x) \in \mathbb{R}^{n}f(x)∈Rn为向量

  • x∈Rx \in \mathbb{R}x∈R为标量（向量-标量）
    记f(x)=[f1(x),f2(x),⋯ ,fn(x)]f(x) = [f_1(x), f_2(x), \cdots, f_n(x)]f(x)=[f1​(x),f2​(x),⋯,fn​(x)]，其中fi,fjf_i, f_jfi​,fj​相互独立。则
    [df(x)dx]i=dfi(x)dx\begin{equation} \left[\frac{d f(x)}{d x} \right]_i = \frac{d f_i(x)}{d x} \end{equation}[dxdf(x)​]i​=dxdfi​(x)​​​
    为向量。即
    df(x)dx=[df1(x)dxdf2(x)dx⋮dfn(x)dx]\begin{equation} \frac{d f(x)}{d x} = \left[ \begin{array} {c} \frac{d f_1(x)}{d x} \\ \frac{d f_2(x)}{d x} \\ \vdots\\ \frac{d f_n(x)}{d x} \end{array} \right] \end{equation}dxdf(x)​=⎣⎡​dxdf1​(x)​dxdf2​(x)​⋮dxdfn​(x)​​⎦⎤​​​

  • x∈Rm\mathbf{x} \in \mathbb{R}^mx∈Rm为向量（向量-向量）
    记f(x)=[f1(x),f2(x),⋯ ,fn(x)]Tf(\mathbf{x}) = [f_1(\mathbf{x}), f_2(\mathbf{x}), \cdots, f_n(\mathbf{x})]^Tf(x)=[f1​(x),f2​(x),⋯,fn​(x)]T；其中fi(x)∈Rf_i(\mathbf{x}) \in \mathbb{R}fi​(x)∈R，fi(x),fj(x)f_i(\mathbf{x}), f_j(\mathbf{x})fi​(x),fj​(x)相互独立。则
    [dfdx]ij=∂fi(x)∂xj\begin{equation} \left[ \frac{d f}{d \mathbf{x}} \right]_{ij} = \frac{ \partial f_i(\mathbf{x})}{\partial \mathbf{x}_j} \end{equation}[dxdf​]ij​=∂xj​∂fi​(x)​​​
    为矩阵。
    即
    dfdX=[∂f1∂x1∂f1∂x2⋯∂f1∂xn∂f2∂x1∂f2∂x2⋯∂f2∂xn⋮⋮⋯⋮∂fn∂x1∂fn∂x2⋯∂fn∂xn]\begin{equation} \frac{df}{d\mathbf{X}} = \left[ \begin{array}{c} \frac{\partial f_1}{\partial x_1} & \frac{\partial f_1}{\partial x_2} & \cdots & \frac{\partial f_1}{\partial x_n} \\ \frac{\partial f_2}{\partial x_1} & \frac{\partial f_2}{\partial x_2} & \cdots & \frac{\partial f_2}{\partial x_n} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \cdots & \vdots\\ \frac{\partial f_n}{\partial x_1} & \frac{\partial f_n}{\partial x_2} & \cdots & \frac{\partial f_n}{\partial x_n} \end{array} \right] \end{equation}dXdf​=⎣⎡​∂x1​∂f1​​∂x1​∂f2​​⋮∂x1​∂fn​​​∂x2​∂f1​​∂x2​∂f2​​⋮∂x2​∂fn​​​⋯⋯⋯⋯​∂xn​∂f1​​∂xn​∂f2​​⋮∂xn​∂fn​​​⎦⎤​​​
    该矩阵称为Jacobian matrix。
